Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 June 2026

Clipper Poster ("the App") is a personal tool operated by its owner to upload the owner's own short-form video clips to the owner's own TikTok account through TikTok's official Content Posting API. This policy explains what data the App handles.

Information we access

When you authorize the App through TikTok Login, TikTok provides the App with an access token and your basic profile information (such as your TikTok open ID and display name). The App requests only the user.info.basic, video.upload, and video.publish permissions, which it uses solely to upload videos that you choose to your own account.

How we use it

Access tokens are used only to authenticate uploads to your own TikTok account. The App does not read your feed, messages, followers, or any other account data beyond what is needed to post a video.

Storage and sharing

Access tokens and basic profile data are stored locally on the owner's own computer. The App has no server, does not transmit your data to any third party, and does not sell, rent, or share your information with anyone.

Retention

Tokens remain on the local machine until you revoke the App's access in your TikTok account settings or delete the local credentials file. Revoking access immediately ends the App's ability to act on your behalf.

Your control

You can revoke access at any time from your TikTok account's connected-apps settings. You may also delete the local credentials file to remove all stored tokens.
